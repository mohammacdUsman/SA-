Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of legal consultation do you provide?

I provide consultations in blockchain legal frameworks, digital currency regulation, corporate governance, and intellectual property protection in digital environments. My expertise spans both theoretical legal research and practical implementation.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Yes, I regularly participate in conferences, workshops, and educational events on topics related to blockchain technology, digital law, and corporate governance. Please provide details about your event when making an inquiry.

Do you collaborate on academic research projects?

I am actively interested in research collaborations that align with my areas of expertise, particularly those at the intersection of law, technology, and business. I have experience in interdisciplinary research and welcome innovative project proposals.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

I typically respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. For urgent matters, please indicate the time-sensitive nature of your request in the subject line of your message.
